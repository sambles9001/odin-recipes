Strawberry Milk

Description

Did you have one when you were a child in the paper cartons? This recipe takes that beverage and brings it to life the proper way. Real strawberries, plain and simple.

Ingredients

  • half and half milk
  • strawberries
  • sugar

Lets make this tasty drink/dessert

  1. wash the strawberries and cut into small bits and place in a bowl
  2. add sugar to starberries and mix, then cover and set aside until sugar dissolves
  3. scoop a good size amount into a cup
  4. add half and half, stir and enjoy!
