Pizza Pie

Decription

Yet another classic from Italy. Perhaps the most popular dish in the world, Pizza. This recipe is as simple as it gets when it comes to pizza and we are making it from scratch. Simple ingredients but amazing flavors. Enjoy this recipe and make it with love.

Ingredients

  • all purpose flour
  • olive oil
  • canned san marzano tomatoes
  • dry yeast
  • mozzerella chees
  • fresh basil

Lets Make A Pizza Pie!

  1. activate the yeast in lukewarm water
  2. add water and a few drizzles of oil to your flour so that a dough can be formed, you want the dough to be a little sticky
  3. add the yeast in and incorporate well
  4. cover and leave so that the dough can rise, ideally you can leave it in the fridge overnight
  5. prepare the tomato sauce by crushing the tomatoes
  6. once the dough doubles in size it is ready to be formed into pizza dough balls and once you have a ball use your hands to work it into a flat pizza shape
  7. apply the sauce over the dough and then the cheese
  8. place in oven until the cheese is melted and the crusted starts to brown
  9. take out of oven and add fresh basil on top as well as a drizzle of olive oil!
